About

I am a second year D.Phil. (Ph.D.) candidate in the Machine Learning Research Group at the University of Oxford, supervised by Michael A Osborne.
I am funded through a Oxford - Google DeepMind Graduate Scholarship.

My research interests within Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence are broad and include topics such as Bayesian frameworks for Deep Learning, Bayesian Neural Networks and Gaussian Processes.
Furthermore, lately I have been looking at the intersections of Machine Learning, Physics and High Performance Computing.

My CV is available for download.
